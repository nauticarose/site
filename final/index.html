Personal Statement

Me

My artwork centers on understanding how social traditions affect social and civil rights. Usually women in general inspire my art, I am a hard core feminist and I like the idea of being able to show how strong and powerful a women could/can be with art. I want to raise awareness as to how this cultural practice has silently, but powerfully influenced the condition of people's parity, representation, and treatment. I love art, it's something that has been instilled in me since I was young and the fact that I can continue and carry this love in my life is something that is really important to me. As well as the fact that There's something about being able to capture a certain movement or a certain figure in art that is just so captivating, and unreal.
